Workshop Days

Do you want to try your hand at ceramics - to see if you can make a pot on a wheel or build a pot or sculpture?

We have a ceramic studio situated between Lincoln and Market Rasen in a rural location. The studio has two pottery wheels and is ideal for a single person or for two people to visit and “have a go”. Most work in the studio is done using stoneware clay and a variety of glazes. Tuition is provided to help you experience the process of throwing or building your “piece” which can then be glazed and fired. If you are interested, please get in touch by e-mail.

3 Hour sessions for two people are charged at £50, for one person £30.
A follow on session can then be arranged for glazing turning etc if wished.
